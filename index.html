About Us

Who We Are...

ngSupplyChain (pronounced as NextGen Supply Chain) is a startup in operation since November, 2018 based out of Bangalore with a team of dedicated professionals solving challenges around B2B inventory fulfilment.

Our services include omni-channel fulfillment strategy, solution design and implementation, managed services, project and program management consulting, and business process consulting.

Product

What We Do...

The first product from ng is a Warehouse Management System (ngWMS) that shall be a completely SaaS (Software-as-a-Service) based inventory and fulfilment solution for small and medium enterprises looking for a robust application with plug and play feature capability and extremely fast deployment that translates to reduced total cost of ownership.

The expected release date for this product is Q4 2021.

Services

How we do...

Given our extensive experience in 3PL and Retail domain, we also work with customers to understand their challenges and design custom solutions that are easy to deploy and provide quick resolution to their current challenges.

Our consulting engagements are primarily driven by business challenges and not restricted to any technology or solution footprint in the client organisation.
